Get to know me

Since 1994, I've thrown myself into all things technology and the internet. Even when I went to study at the University of Manchester for my Physics degree, I still managed to come back to it - and it's there I taught myself to program.

I currently work as a cybersecurity associate, but I spend a lot of my free time working on personal websites and programming challenges.
